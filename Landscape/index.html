Cosmic Facts

Explore a few fascinating truths about our magnificent universe. These cosmic facts remind us how vast, mysterious, and beautiful the cosmos truly is.

Age of the Universe

Approximately 13.8 billion years old, the universe holds billions of stories written in its cosmic fabric.

Stellar Abundance

There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on all Earth’s beaches—a cosmic sea of lights.

Observable Diameter

The observable universe spans about 93 billion light years, offering an endless horizon of mystery.

Mysterious Dark Matter

Only about 5% of the cosmos is luminous; the rest is dark matter and dark energy, unseen but profoundly influential.
